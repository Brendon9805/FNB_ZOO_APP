🌳 THE LOST FOREST

Step into The Lost Forest — a serene escape hidden deep in the eastern side of the zoo. Surrounded by ancient trees, singing birds, and calm lakeside breezes, this magical picnic area offers a peaceful retreat from the buzz of the animal enclosures.

🍃 WHAT YOU'LL ENJOY

  • 🪵 Rustic picnic tables beneath forest canopy
  • 🦜 Spot native birds like hornbills and kingfishers
  • 📚 Quiet corners perfect for reading or sketching
  • 🚻 Clean and eco-friendly restrooms nearby
  • 🧺 Family-friendly picnic zones with waste bins
  • 🌅 Beautiful lake views, especially at sunset

📍 LOCATION

You'll find The Lost Forest beyond Monkey Trail on the eastern edge of the zoo. Follow the path marked with green leaf signs 🍃. It's about a 5-minute walk from the Tropical Aviary.

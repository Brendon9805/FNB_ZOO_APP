🍽️ DINE @THE ZOO

Gourmet meals, a serene atmosphere, live jazz, and good company — sometimes, that's all you need. Dine @The Zoo offers an elevated culinary experience surrounded by natural beauty and a touch of wildlife charm.

✨ WHY YOU'LL LOVE IT

  • 🍷 Fine dining menu curated by top local chefs
  • 🎶 Live music on weekends featuring jazz & acoustic artists
  • 🌿 Outdoor and indoor seating with ambient lighting
  • 🧑‍🍳 Vegan, vegetarian & kids' options available
  • 🍰 Desserts to die for – including the zoo’s famous chocolate lava cake
🌙 “A magical night under the stars, with the sounds of the wild in the distance.”

📍 WHERE TO FIND IT

Dine @The Zoo is located just behind the Amphitheatre. Follow the lantern-lit path after 5 PM for a special night experience.

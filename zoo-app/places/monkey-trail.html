🐒 MONKEY TRAIL ADVENTURE

Welcome to one of the zoo's most exciting attractions! The Monkey Trail is a dynamic outdoor experience where visitors can explore the playful and wild world of monkeys.

🎯 WHAT TO EXPECT

  • 🌿 Choose between a 30-minute or a 1-hour trail
  • 🐾 Spot different monkey species in their natural habitat
  • 🚴‍♀️ Walk or rent a mountain bike to ride the trail
  • 📸 Scenic jungle paths, perfect for photos

⚠️ Trails can get muddy — wear comfortable clothes and shoes you don’t mind getting dirty!

🧒 WHO CAN JOIN?

The trail is suitable for all ages, but children under 12 must be accompanied by an adult. There are rest spots every 15 minutes and emergency exits along the route.

📍 WHERE TO FIND IT

The Monkey Trail is located on the eastern side of the zoo, just past the Tropical Aviary. Follow the green signs marked with monkey footprints 🐾.

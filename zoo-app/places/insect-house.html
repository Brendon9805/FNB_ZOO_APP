🪲 WELCOME TO THE INSECT HOUSE

Enter the miniature world of the planet’s most fascinating creatures. The Insect House features one of the world’s most expansive collections of bugs — from rare rainforest insects to ancient arachnids and dazzling butterflies.

🧬 DISCOVER INCREDIBLE INSECTS

  • 🦋 Wander through the Butterfly Dome — home to over 30 free-flying species
  • 🐜 Watch Leafcutter Ants build colonies in real-time glass tunnels
  • 🕷️ Meet our Giant Tarantula “Shadow” in the nocturnal zone
  • 🪳 Hold a hissing cockroach (if you dare!)
  • 🔍 Examine wings, eyes & exoskeletons under real microscopes
  • 🎥 Enjoy short documentaries in the Bug Theater
🧠 Did you know? Insects make up over 80% of all known animal species!

📍 WHERE TO FIND IT

You’ll find the Insect House on the eastern side of the zoo, just next to Monkey Trail. Follow the buzzing signs marked 🐞 and keep your eyes peeled!
